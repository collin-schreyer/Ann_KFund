### TOASTS
**7:45 PM**

**Welcome and Toast to the Republic of Franconia**  
*The President of the United States*

The President will welcome Prime Minister Dubois and celebrate the enduring friendship between the United States and Franconia, highlighting recent cooperation on climate initiatives and economic partnership.

**Response and Toast to the United States**  
*Prime Minister Jean-Claude Dubois*

The Prime Minister will express gratitude for American hospitality and discuss shared values and future collaboration.

**Remarks on Bilateral Relations**  
*The Secretary of State*

The Secretary will provide remarks on the diplomatic relationship and announce new cultural exchange programs between our nations.
